Clarified butter

Clarified butter has a more concentrated, richer flavor, lasts longer in the refrigerator, and has a higher smoke point for cooking.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound unsalted butter

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan,over low heat warm butter without boiling or aggitation.
  2. Once the butter begins to melt the solids will float to the top and the water will settle on the bottom.
  3. When the butter is melted remove the milk solids using a ladle.
  4. After all the solids are removed transfer the butterfat to another container leaving the water in the pan.
